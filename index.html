Why do you want to be a web developer?

I looked into web developing as it checked the boxes for almost, if not, all the boxes in an enjoyable career choice for me. I mentioned how I love challenges and adventure. I tend to get bored easily of repetitive tasks especially ones where the problem and solution are fairly simple or can be learned with little to no effort. Previous to the disaster that was quarantine, I was a Special Education teacher. The students ALWAYS kept me on my toes, however contrary to popular belief, being a teacher meant a lot more than just being with the students, specifically in special education. It was long hours, legal paperwork, 2-3 hour IEP meetings, many tasks to be done OUTSIDE of our contracted time. Because it was 65% geared towards these mundane tasks and only 40% of my effort was towards my students, I felt unmotivated. Web developing so far seems to keep me focused, even in the prework, which I usually find to be tedious in other settings. In most modules I have found a little obstacle that has completely thrown me for a loop, but has been fun to figure out. From speaking to friends who are also in the field this is a frequent occurrence. I am hopeful that SWE industry will keep me motivated and hungry for me!
